# Dual Straight Pull Assembly

## Steps

### Step 1
On the front of the PrimeBlock, insert a 6-32 Hex Nut into the cutout on the inside and screw in a 6-32 ⁵⁄₁₆”  Phillips Screw from the outside to secure it, making sure it goes through the Pump Bar. Repeat on the other side on the front of the PrimeBlock.

### Step 2
On the back of the PrimeBlock, insert a 6-32 Hex Nut into the cutout on the inside and screw in a 6-32 ⁵⁄₁₆”  Phillips Screw from the outside to secure it, making sure it goes through the Pump Bar. Repeat on the other side on the back of the PrimeBlock.

![Step 2 image 1](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-02_picture1.png)
![Step 2 image 2](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-02_picture2.png)

![Step 2 image 4](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-02_picture4.png)

### Step 3
Insert a reinforcing 6-32 ⅝” Socket Head Screw into the top back on each side of the PrimeBlock. Make sure it is fully inserted, but do not overtighten.

![Step 3 image 1](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-03_picture1.png)
![Step 3 image 2](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-03_picture2.png)

![Step 3 image 4](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-03_picture4.png)

### Step 4
Insert (2) reinforcing 6-32 2" Phillips Head Screw into each handle. Make sure it is fully inserted, but do not overtighten.

![Step 4 image 1](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-04_picture1.png)
![Step 4 image 2](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-04_picture2.png)
![Step 4 image 3](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-04_picture3.png)
![Step 4 image 4](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-04_picture4.png)

### Step 5
Slide (2) HandleHead of your choice onto each handle on the PrimeBlock, making sure the attachment hole is facing down. Use a 6-32 ⅛” Socket Head Screw to secure it to the handle, again making sure not to overtighten.

![Step 5 image 1](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-05_picture1.png)
![Step 5 image 2](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-05_picture2.png)

![Step 5 image 5](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-05_picture5.png)
![Step 5 image 6](../../media/prime-block/dual-straight-pull-step-05_picture6.png)

### Step 6
On the bottom of the PrimeBlock, attach a 623 POM Bearing with an 8mm M3 Socket Head Screw, making sure the bearing is securely in place without overtightening. Repeat on the other side.
