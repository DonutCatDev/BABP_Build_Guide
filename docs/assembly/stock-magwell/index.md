# Stock/Magwell Assembly

[View Materials List](materials.md)

## Steps

### Step 1
Note: This step is optional. The StockButt can be used as is without StockButtBackplate. When printing StockButt out of a soft filament, e.g. TPU, however the StockButtBackplate can be useful to add rigidity and prevent the StockButt from peeling off the Stock and to have a more secure attachment point.

### Step 2
Apply a thin bead of super glue around the groove on the back side of StockButt (first photo). Take the StockButtBackplate and line it up with StockButt, making sure the holes align (second photo). Press the two pieces together, ensuring the ridge on StockButtBackplate fits into the groove in StockButt and the glue makes solid contact (third photo). Set aside for 15 minutes for the glue to set, clamping or weighting down to maintain full contact if needed.

### Step 3
Insert ReleaseNub into the backside of Stock, making sure the angled front side is facing down (first photo). Insert the 0.625" Long Compression Spring into the hole on the backside of ReleaseNub (second photo). Take the StockButt (and StockButtSpacer if using) and line it up with the back of the Stock, using (2) 6-32 ⅝”  Phillips Screws to secure it in place (third photo).

![Step 3 image 1](../../media/stock-magwell/stock-magwell-step-03_picture1.png)
![Step 3 image 2](../../media/stock-magwell/stock-magwell-step-03_picture2.png)
![Step 3 image 3](../../media/stock-magwell/stock-magwell-step-03_picture3.png)

### Step 4
Take the Release and line it up with the Magwell, making sure the larger side is facing down (first photo). Spreading open the split side, slide the Release over the front of the Magwell until it closes back into place (second photo).

![Step 4 image 1](../../media/stock-magwell/stock-magwell-step-04_picture1.png)
![Step 4 image 2](../../media/stock-magwell/stock-magwell-step-04_picture2.png)

### Step 5
Slide the Magwell onto the front of the Stock (first photo). Make sure the retaining shoulder aligns with the cutout (second photo), and the split side of the Release slides over the front of the ReleaseNub (third photo).

![Step 5 image 1](../../media/stock-magwell/stock-magwell-step-05_picture1.png)
![Step 5 image 2](../../media/stock-magwell/stock-magwell-step-05_picture2.png)
![Step 5 image 3](../../media/stock-magwell/stock-magwell-step-05_picture3.png)

### Step 6
Continue sliding the Magwell into Stock as far as it goes (first photo). Then, confirm that pushing the front of the Release retracts the ReleaseNub inside of the Magwell, and that the spring pushes it back out when released (second animated photo).
