# Railgun Bipod Variant Final Assembly

## Steps

### Step 1
Install a 6-32  ¼” Heat Set Insert into RGB_Insert, making sure it sits flush and does not protrude. Insert the RGB_Insert flat side up into the RGB_MainBody, pushing it in as far as it will go.

### Step 2
Insert the longer RGB_TopSupportBar and the shorter RGB_BottomSupportBar into the respective slots in the rear of RGB_MainBody. Depending on print tolerances, they may require gentle encouragement with a hammer. Make sure they are inserted fully. Then screw in a 6-32 ⁵⁄₁₆”  Phillips Screw into the bottom of RGB_MainBody to lock RGB_Insert and RGB_BottomSupportBar into place.

![Step 2 image 1](../../media/shroud/bipod-step-02_picture1.png)
![Step 2 image 2](../../media/shroud/bipod-step-02_picture2.png)

### Step 3
Install RGB_Base to RGB_MainBody, making sure the support bars seat correctly within the base then secure with (5) 6-32 ⅝“ Socket Head Screws, being careful not to overtighten.

![Step 3 image 1](../../media/shroud/bipod-step-03_picture1.png)
![Step 3 image 2](../../media/shroud/bipod-step-03_picture2.png)

### Step 4
Slide in Bipod Sub Assembly into the front of RGB_MainBody, making sure the Legs slot into the grooves and are flush with the sides. Then, use a 6-32 ⅝“ Socket Head Screw to connect the Bipod Sub Assembly to the RGB_Insert in its Heat Set Insert. Make sure it goes through the Button locking it in place, and is not installed too tightly such that the Bipod can swivel in the next step. Loctite is recommended.

![Step 4 image 1](../../media/shroud/bipod-step-04_picture1.png)
![Step 4 image 2](../../media/shroud/bipod-step-04_picture2.png)
![Step 4 image 3](../../media/shroud/bipod-step-04_picture3.png)

### Step 5
Confirm the Legs can be pulled out with a bit of force to overcome the soft lock, and that the LockNubs extend behind the Legs when fully extended hard locking them into place. Confirm the Bipod can then swivel side to side, which may require loosening the 6-32 ⅝“ Socket Head Screw installed in the last step. To close the Bipod, press the Button on the front to retract the LockNubs so the Legs can be returned to their closed position.
