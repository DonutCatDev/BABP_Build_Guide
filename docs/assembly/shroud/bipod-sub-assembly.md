# Bipod Sub Assembly

## Steps

### Step 1
Insert the RightLeg into the right side of the Core, then secure it with a 6-32 ⅝“ Socket Head Screw. Make sure the indent in the RightLeg faces inwards, otherwise you may have put it on the wrong side.

### Step 2
Insert the LeftLeg on the left side of the Core, and secure it with a 6-32 ⅝“ Socket Head Screw. Again confirm the indents in both Legs face inward.

![Step 2 image 1](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-02_picture1.png)
![Step 2 image 2](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-02_picture2.png)
![Step 2 image 3](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-02_picture3.png)

### Step 3
Optionally sand/file the front angled section of RightLockNub and LeftLockNub smooth (first photo), this will help make the locking assembly smoother and shorten the breaking in process. Then take RightLockNub and LeftLockNub and place them in Cap, nub side facing into the Cap. Finally, compress and place a Spring in the center between RightLockNub and LeftLockNub.

![Step 3 image 1](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-03_picture1.png)
![Step 3 image 2](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-03_picture2.png)
![Step 3 image 3](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-03_picture3.png)

### Step 4
Take the Cap and LockNubs and place it on the bottom of the Core. Secure it with a 6-32 ⁵⁄₁₆”  Phillips Screw on the back of the Cap. Confirm the LockNubs can move freely, and spring back to the outside position when released.

![Step 4 image 1](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-04_picture1.png)
![Step 4 image 1](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-04_picture1.gif)

### Step 5
Optionally, sand or file smooth the inner center edges of the Button (first photo), as these will slide against RightLockNub and LeftLockNub. Then take (2) 3/32 in by  ¾ in Dowels and insert them into the left and right holes in the Button.

![Step 5 image 1](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-05_picture1.png)
![Step 5 image 2](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-05_picture2.png)
![Step 5 image 3](../../media/shroud/bipod-sub-assembly-step-05_picture3.png)

### Step 6
Insert the Button into the front of the Core, making sure the top guides align and the Dowels enter into the Cap. Verify that pushing the Button retracts the left and right LockNub. This will likely be sticky at first, but repeated use should break in the Button until it is smooth and the Spring is able to return the Button to the unpressed position.
